I am a research scientist with expertise in human‑computer interaction and human‑AI interaction.

I have more than 10 years experience working in industry research labs, including Reality Labs Research (Meta), Autodesk Research, and Chatham Labs, an HCI research startup that I co-founded. I have authored 40 peer-reviewed publications, which have been collectively cited over 1100 times, and my work has resulted in 17 patents.

My research includes work on user interfaces for generative AI, adaptive and intelligent user interfaces, and interactive help and learning systems. My primary research approach involves designing, prototyping, and evaluating novel interactive systems. In the process, I employ quantitative and qualitative study methods, human-centered design, and data science techniques.
